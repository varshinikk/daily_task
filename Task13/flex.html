Visvesvaraya Technological University
Visvesvaraya Technological University is named after notable Indian engineer, scholar, statesman and the recipient of highest honour ‘Bharata Ratna’ Sir. M. Visvesvaraya. VTU is an institution that is rich in history, academic excellence, and leadership. The University is performing and continues to perform with a VISION, MISSION and MANDATE.Visvesvaraya Technological university is one of India’s leading and largest technological Universities.
It has 218 affiliated engineering colleges under its jurisdiction.Equipped with the extremely rich resources of an outstanding student body and faculty strong partnerships with business, industry, and government and support from alumni and friends.
