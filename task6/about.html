The Massachusetts Institute of Technology is a private research university in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Founded in 1861 in response to the increasing industrialization of the United States, MIT adopted a European polytechnic university model and stressed laboratory instruction in applied science and engineering.

Our college is committed for national and social cause by organizing blood donation camp. To cater the academic and research-oriented needs of the students and staff, our college organizes conferences, workshops and training programmes. Along with academic achievements, our college contributes to develop the overall personality of the students by organizing co-curricular and extracurricular activities such as Technical paper presentation competition, Sports Competitions and students are motivated to participate in Cultural events and to contribute in annual college magazine “Cauvery Jnana Dhare”. I hope our college will prosper academically in future.
